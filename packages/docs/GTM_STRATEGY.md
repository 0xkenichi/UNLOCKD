# Vestra Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

This document outlines the strategic path for Vestra (Vestra) to achieve market dominance in the vested token liquidity space.

## 1. High-Level Objectives

- **User Growth:** Attract 10,000+ active testnet users within 90 days.
- **Institutional Validation:** Secure 5+ Pilot LOIs from tier-1 DAOs and startups.
- **Community Engagement:** Convert testnet activity into long-termprotocol loyalty via `$Vestra` airdrop incentives.
- **Ecosystem Integration:** Secure grants and accelerator support (e.g., Base, Avalanche, Solana).

---

## 2. Market Segmentation

| Segment | Pain Point | Vestra Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **DAO Contributors** | Paper wealth (vesting) but cash poor. | Non-custodial borrowing against claims. |
| **Treasury Teams** | Need to support talent without diluting or selling early. | Self-repaying loan infrastructure for staff. |
| **Institutional Lenders** | Searching for low-volatility, secured yield. | Deterministic, time-based collateral (no price liquidation). |
| **Speculative Traders** | Limited ways to hedge illiquid vesting positions. | Synthetic hedging and OTC auction defaults. |

---

## 3. Positioning & Messaging Pillars

- **"Vesting-Friendly Credit":** No forced sales. Preserve your upside while accessing liquidity.
- **"Privacy as a Primitive":** Stealth addresses protect user flows from public scrutiny.
- **"The Anti-Margin Call Layer":** Risk is managed via time (unlock) rather than volatile spot prices.

---

## 4. Growth Channels & Tactics

### Phase 1: Testnet & Airdrop (Weeks 1-8)
- **Incentivized Testnet:** Points-based system for borrowing, lending, and testing privacy flows.
- **Viral Loops:** Referral bonuses for onboarding new testers.
- **Developer Bounty:** Small grants for building early adapters for new vesting standards.

### Phase 2: Pilot & Partner (Weeks 9-16)
- **Direct Outreach:** Targeting founders of recently launched projects (post-TGE).
- **Eco-Sponsorships:** Presenting at Base/Optimism/Solana builder houses to secure pilot partners.
- **Transparency Reports:** Weekly KPI snapshots (Loan Volume, TVL, Defaults) to build trust.

### Phase 3: Mainnet Controlled Rollout (Week 17+)
- **Whitelist Access:** Transitioning top testnet participants and pilot partners to a controlled mainnet cohort.
- **Audit-First Marketing:** Leading with security reports and formal verification proofs.

---

## 5. Success Metrics (KPIs)

- **Testnet Wallets:** Target 10k+.
- **Conversion to Mainnet:** Target 20% of testnet users.
- **Default Resolution Rate:** Target 100% (via on-chain enforcement).
- **Institutional LOIs:** Target 2+ signed by Month 3.
