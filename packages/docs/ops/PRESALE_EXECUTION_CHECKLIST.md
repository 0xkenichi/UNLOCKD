# Pre-Sale Execution Checklist

Last updated: 2026-02-13
Owner: Founder office

Use this checklist before opening any pre-sale process.

## Policy Alignment

- [ ] Allocation aligns with `docs/TOKENOMICS_FINAL.md`.
- [ ] Vesting terms documented and standardized.
- [ ] Guardrails for transferability and unlocks defined.

## Compliance and Disclosure

- [ ] Jurisdiction and participant eligibility policy documented.
- [ ] Risk disclosures and non-investment disclaimers included.
- [ ] Recordkeeping process defined for subscriptions and communications.

## Commercial Setup

- [ ] Target raise range and minimum commitment defined.
- [ ] Round timeline and close conditions documented.
- [ ] Investor communication cadence agreed.

## Technical and Operations

- [ ] Public dashboard and KPI evidence package current.
- [ ] Security posture statement current and signed off by owner.
- [ ] Incident response and escalation contact shared.

## Investor Readiness

- [ ] Dataroom index complete: `docs/ops/INVESTOR_DATAROOM_INDEX.md`.
- [ ] Diligence FAQ prepared and reviewed.
- [ ] Commitments tracker template prepared.

## Go/No-Go Decision

- [ ] Critical blockers reviewed and closed or accepted with rationale.
- [ ] Final go/no-go owner sign-off date recorded.
