# Vestra Protocol Branding Guide

This document defines the brand identity of Vestra, ensuring consistency across all communication channels and product interfaces.

## 1. Mission and Vision

### Mission
To democratize liquidity for vested token claims, providing non-custodial, privacy-first credit infrastructure that preserves long-term alignment between builders and ecosystems.

### Vision
Position Vestra as the foundational credit primitive for the future of on-chain work, where every locked asset is liquid, secure, and private.

---

## 2. Brand Identity & Voice

### Brand Personality
- **Sophisticated & Secure:** High-tech, institutional-grade reliability.
- **Disruptive yet Responsible:** Challenging the status quo with conservative risk models.
- **Advocate for Privacy:** Uncompromising on user financial sovereignty.

### Voice and Tone
- **Authoritative:** Speak with technical confidence.
- **Clear:** Avoid jargon where simple explanations suffice (e.g., "Future value into usable credit").
- **Direct:** No fluff. Focus on outcomes: liquidity, security, alignment.

### Canonical Description
> **Vestra (Vestra):** A non-custodial credit protocol for borrowing against locked or vesting token claims using deterministic underwriting and settlement-at-unlock enforcement.

---

## 3. Visual DNA (Suggested)

### Color Palette
- **Vestra Deep Navy:** `#0A0F1E` (Backgrounds, Primary depth)
- **Vestra Neon Cyan:** `#00F2FF` (Primary Action, Highlights)
- **Vestra Obsidian:** `#1A1B23` (Component surfaces)
- **Risk Amber:** `#FFB800` (Warnings, LTV limits)

### Typography
- **Primary:** *Outfit* or *Inter* (Geometric, modern, highly legible)
- **Mono:** *JetBrains Mono* (For technical data and contract addresses)

---

## 4. Messaging Pillars

1. **Unlock the Idle:** Turn non-transferable vesting claims into immediate liquidity.
2. **Preserve Alignment:** Borrow without selling, maintaining your long-term upside in the project.
3. **Privacy by Default:** Opt-in stealth addresses ensure your financial flows remain confidential.
4. **Deterministic Risk:** No arbitrary margin calls; liquidation is tied to time/unlock events, not price volatility.

---

## 5. Usage Guardrails
- **DO:** Use "Vestra (Vestra)" in formal documentation.
- **DO:** Emphasize "Non-custodial" and "Risk-first".
- **DON'T:** Promise "zero risk" or "guaranteed gains".
- **DON'T:** Use generic "DeFi" imagery; lean into "Deep Tech" and "Financial Infrastructure" aesthetics.
