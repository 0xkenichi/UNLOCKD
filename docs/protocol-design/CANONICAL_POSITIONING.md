## Canonical One-Liner

Vestra (Vestra) is a non-custodial credit protocol that lets users borrow against locked or vesting token claims using conservative DPV/LTV underwriting and deterministic settlement at unlock.

## What We Do

- Convert non-transferable, time-locked token value into usable credit.
- Preserve long-term vesting alignment by avoiding forced token sales.
- Protect lenders with conservative risk controls and on-chain settlement enforcement.

## What We Do Not Claim

- We do not custody unlocked borrower tokens in advance.
- We do not run a no-risk lending product.
- We do not treat testnet pilots as production proof without audit and controls.

## Canonical Chain Narrative

1. Local + Sepolia: development, simulation, pilot rehearsal.
2. Base Sepolia: partner-facing testnet pilots and reliability evidence.
3. Mainnet controlled cohort: limited exposure after audit closeout and remediation.
4. Broader mainnet distribution: only after controlled cohort KPIs stay within guardrails.

Rules:
- Avoid claiming "mainnet live" unless controlled cohort is active and documented.
- Avoid conflicting chain claims in docs; if unsure, point to this file.

## Core ICP and Value Messaging

Primary buyers and users:
- DAO contributors with locked/vesting allocations who need liquidity.
- Treasury and finance teams seeking non-dilutive contributor support.
- Lenders seeking time-bounded yield with conservative collateral policies.

Core value points:
- Borrow without breaking vesting schedules.
- Conservative underwriting with transparent parameters.
- Deterministic debt-first settlement at unlock.
