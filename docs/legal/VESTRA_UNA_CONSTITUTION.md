# Constitution of the Vestra Unincorporated Nonprofit Association (UNA)

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** March 14, 2026  
**Status:** Formal Governance Document  

---

## Article I: Name and Purpose

### 1.1 Name
The name of this association shall be the **Vestra Unincorporated Nonprofit Association** (the "Association").

### 1.2 Purpose
The Association is formed for the primary purpose of developing, maintaining, and governing the **Vestra Protocol** (the "Protocol"). The Protocol is intended to serve as a decentralized public utility for credit and liquidity. The Association is organized for the common benefit of the Protocol’s users and the broader decentralized finance ecosystem.

### 1.3 Nonprofit Character
The Association is a nonprofit entity. No part of the net earnings or assets of the Association shall inure to the benefit of, or be distributable to, its members, officers, or other private persons, except to pay reasonable compensation for services rendered or to make payments and distributions in furtherance of the purposes set forth in Article 1.2.

---

## Article II: Membership and Governance

### 2.1 Members
The initial members of the Association are the core contributors and founders of the Vestra Protocol. Membership may be expanded or modified according to the governance rules defined in Article 2.2.

### 2.2 Participation and Voting
Governance of the Association shall be conducted through the Protocol's decentralized governance mechanisms (e.g., `veCRDT` token-based voting). Decisions made through these on-chain mechanisms shall be considered the official decisions of the Association.

### 2.3 Limited Liability
As provided by applicable laws (such as the Uniform Unincorporated Nonprofit Association Act), a member or officer is not personally liable for a debt, obligation, or liability of the Association solely by reason of being a member or officer.

---

## Article III: Assets and Dissolution

### 3.1 Association Assets
Any assets held by the Association, including intellectual property, treasury funds, and contract rights, shall be used exclusively to further the Purpose defined in Article 1.2.

### 3.2 Dissolution
Upon the dissolution of the Association, assets remaining after the payment of all debts and liabilities shall be distributed to one or more nonprofit organizations or decentralized entities with a similar purpose, or as directed by the governance mechanism in Article 2.2, provided such distribution aligns with nonprofit regulations.

---

## Article IV: Adoption

By adopting this Constitution, the Association is formally established as a legal entity under the principles of the Unincorporated Nonprofit Association framework.
