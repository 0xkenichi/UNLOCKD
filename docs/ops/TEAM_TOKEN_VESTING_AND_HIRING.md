# Team Token Vesting and Hiring Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-13
Owner: Founder office

This policy defines how to hire core roles using milestone-based vesting aligned to tokenomics.

## Purpose

- Attract high-leverage early contributors before full cash-market hiring is possible.
- Keep incentives aligned with long-term protocol outcomes.
- Preserve treasury discipline and transparent governance controls.

## Role Priorities (First Three Hires)

1. Protocol engineer (smart contracts + security)
2. Full-stack engineer (frontend/backend reliability)
3. Partnerships lead (pilot conversion and GTM distribution)

## Compensation Structure

- Cash: conservative base within runway limits
- Token grant: role and level based
- Vesting: milestone-gated + time-based hybrid

## Vesting Policy Guardrails

- Default team vesting baseline references:
  - `docs/TOKENOMICS_FINAL.md`
- Every grant must include:
  - cliff period
  - vesting horizon
  - acceleration conditions (if any)
  - termination clauses

## Milestone-Based Vesting Framework

Each grant has:
- Time vesting component
- Milestone unlock component

Milestones should map to measurable outcomes, for example:
- Security: audit remediation delivered
- Product: pilot conversion and reliability targets hit
- GTM: signed LOIs and paid pilot conversions

## Governance and Transparency

- All token grants tracked in internal cap table ledger.
- Quarterly disclosure of grant totals and remaining pool availability.
- No ad hoc allocation changes without documented approval process.

## Hiring Pipeline SLA

- Role brief approved before sourcing
- Candidate scorecard used for every interview
- Offer package includes cash, token, vesting, and expectations
- Offer to acceptance target <= 14 days

## Quarterly Review

- Role mix against roadmap needs
- Grant efficiency vs. outcomes delivered
- Pool health and runway impact
