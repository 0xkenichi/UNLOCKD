# Funding and Pre-Sale Sprint Plan

Last updated: 2026-02-13
Owner: Founder office

This sprint converts pilot and security evidence into funding outcomes.

## Sprint Goal (6 Weeks)

- 20 investor meetings completed
- 5 active diligence processes
- 1 lead term-sheet path or pre-sale soft commitments >=30% of target

## Workstreams

1. Narrative and materials
- Keep one canonical story using `docs/CANONICAL_POSITIONING.md`.
- Maintain one investor memo source:
  - `docs/INVESTOR_MEMO_ONE_PAGER.md`
- Add KPI evidence from weekly pilot reports.

2. Dataroom readiness
- Product and architecture docs
- Security posture and incident controls
- Tokenomics and treasury policy
- Pilot KPI evidence and partner references
- Dataroom checklist:
  - `docs/ops/INVESTOR_DATAROOM_INDEX.md`

3. Investor pipeline
- Prioritize investors with DeFi credit and infra thesis fit.
- Track stage progression weekly.

4. Pre-sale parallel path
- Define eligibility policy and allocation mechanics.
- Keep vesting terms aligned with `docs/TOKENOMICS_FINAL.md`.
- Execution checklist:
  - `docs/ops/PRESALE_EXECUTION_CHECKLIST.md`

## Weekly Cadence

- Monday: investor target and intro sprint planning
- Tuesday-Thursday: meetings and follow-ups
- Friday: diligence status update and risk log

## KPI Dashboard

- Meetings booked/completed
- Diligence processes opened
- Follow-up cycle time
- Soft commitments amount
- Top blockers with owner and ETA

## No-Go Signals

- Repeated narrative confusion on naming/chain claims
- Missing security ownership or unresolved critical incidents
- Missing pilot evidence for core product claims

## End-of-Sprint Deliverables

- Updated memo with current evidence
- Diligence FAQ v1
- Funding recommendation memo:
  - raise now
  - extend pilot proof
  - run pre-sale first
