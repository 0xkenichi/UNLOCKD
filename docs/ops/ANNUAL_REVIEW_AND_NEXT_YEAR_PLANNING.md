# Annual Review and Next-Year Planning Framework

Last updated: 2026-02-13
Owner: Founder office

Use this framework to close the year with auditable outcomes and define the next 12-month operating plan.

## Year-End Deliverables

- Annual outcomes report
- KPI performance summary by quarter
- Security and incident summary
- Funding and treasury status summary
- Next-year roadmap with budget and hiring envelope

## Annual Scorecard

Track outcomes in five sections:

1. Growth
- Active partners and pilot conversions
- Active lenders/borrowers
- Loan volume trend

2. Product
- Funnel conversion trend
- Time-to-loan and completion reliability
- User retention trend

3. Risk and Security
- Incident counts by severity
- Audit findings opened/closed
- Bad debt trend and mitigation outcomes

4. Business
- Fee revenue and runway trajectory
- Pilot-to-paid conversion rate
- Capital raised and financing optionality

5. Execution
- Milestone hit rate
- Release cadence
- PR cycle time and quality gate pass rate

## Planning Process (4 Weeks)

Week 1:
- Collect annual data and close missing metrics

Week 2:
- Run retro by workstream (product, GTM, security, funding)

Week 3:
- Draft next-year goals and budget scenarios

Week 4:
- Finalize roadmap, owners, and quarterly checkpoints

## Next-Year Plan Requirements

- Clear top-3 strategic outcomes
- Quarterly milestones with KPIs
- Budget envelope and hiring assumptions
- Dependency and risk register
- Trigger conditions for reprioritization

## Definition of Done

- Annual report published internally
- Next-year roadmap approved by owner
- Q1 kickoff priorities and owners assigned
