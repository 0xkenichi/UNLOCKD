# GTM 90-Day Execution Plan

Last updated: 2026-02-13
Owner: Founder office

This plan operationalizes the targets in `docs/GTM_PIPELINE.md` for a solo founder setup.

## Outcomes

- 15+ qualified partner conversations
- 5 pilot-ready counterparties
- 2 signed pilot LOIs
- 1 live named pilot with published weekly KPI report

## Weekly Cadence (12 Weeks)

Every week:
- Monday: pipeline review and outreach planning
- Tuesday-Thursday: partner calls and follow-ups
- Friday: scorecard update and blockers review

## Stage Definitions and Exit Criteria

1. Prospect
   - Has active vesting population and treasury motivation
   - Exit: first discovery call booked
2. Qualified
   - Technical and legal viability confirmed
   - Exit: pilot scope draft accepted
3. Pilot Design
   - Success KPIs and operating contacts agreed
   - Exit: LOI draft shared and negotiated
4. LOI Signed
   - Non-binding intent recorded
   - Exit: launch checklist date committed
5. Pilot Live
   - First weekly KPI report published
   - Exit: month-end pilot review delivered

## Weekly Targets by Month

Month 1 (Weeks 1-4):
- Build target list of 50 accounts
- Book 10 qualified conversations
- Advance at least 3 accounts to Pilot Design

Month 2 (Weeks 5-8):
- Reach 15+ total qualified conversations
- Convert 5 accounts to pilot-ready
- Sign 2 LOIs

Month 3 (Weeks 9-12):
- Launch 1 named pilot
- Publish weekly KPI snapshots
- Produce one pilot case-study draft

## CRM Template (Use in Notion/Airtable/Sheet)

Required columns:
- Account name
- Ecosystem/chain
- Contact owner
- Stage
- Last touch date
- Next action date
- Technical fit (high/medium/low)
- Legal fit (high/medium/low)
- Pilot potential score (1-5)
- Blocker summary

## KPI Scorecard

Track weekly:
- New prospects added
- Discovery calls completed
- Qualified opportunities
- Pilot-ready opportunities
- LOIs sent
- LOIs signed
- Days in stage (median)
- Pilot launch date confidence (0-100)

## Risks and Countermeasures

- Low response rates:
  - Use warm intros and ecosystem operators first.
- Legal friction:
  - Provide non-binding LOI and pilot-only scopes.
- Slow technical confidence:
  - Run guided testnet demos with partner wallet + sample vesting.

## Artifacts Required by End of Day Friday

- Updated CRM
- KPI scorecard snapshot
- Blocker log with owner and due date
- Next-week priority list (top 3)
