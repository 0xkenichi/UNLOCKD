# Credit Markets for Time-Locked Digital Assets
## Litepaper (v1.0 - Public Release)

Date: March 13, 2026  
Authors: Vestra Protocol Team

### Summary
Web3 vesting and lockups protect long-term alignment but trap billions in illiquid value. Vestra Protocol enables borrowing against the deterministic value of time-locked tokens without transferring ownership or breaking vesting rules. Loans are issued non-custodially, settle automatically at unlock, and preserve user privacy while maintaining absolute lender safety.

### Problem
- **Inert Capital**: Locked tokens have market value but zero immediate utility.
- **Liquidation Scarcity**: Existing DeFi lending requires liquid collateral and risks price-based liquidation.
- **Market Haircuts**: OTC sales of future claims often force 50-70% discounts.

### Solution: Vestra Credit
Borrow against vested token claims, valued conservatively via our Dynamic Discounted Present Value (dDPV) engine:

`PV = Q * P * D`

Where `Q` is quantity, `P` is oracle price, and `D` is our composite risk discount.

### Key Features
- **Non-Custodial Escrow**: Maintain control while securing credit.
- **Deterministic Enforcement**: On-chain settlement at the moment of unlock.
- **Zero-Margin Call Risk**: Risk is managed by time and protocol maturity, not spot volatility.

### Loan Lifecycle
1. **Escrow**: Claim rights are locked via a Vestra adapter.
2. **Borrow**: Receive stablecoins against the dDPV.
3. **Settle**: Automatic principal and interest recovery at unlock.
4. **Release**: Residual tokens are returned to the borrower post-settlement.

### Conclusion
Vestra Protocol converts locked token value into usable liquidity while preserving vesting integrity. It is the fundamental credit layer for the future of on-chain work.
