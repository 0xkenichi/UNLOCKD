# Risk Simulation Models

Vestra Protocol utilizes deterministic risk modeling to quantify uncertainty and protect protocol liquidity. Our approach combines real-time on-chain discounting with periodic off-chain stress testing (Monte Carlo simulations) to calibrate global protocol parameters.

## 1. The dDPV Underwriting Engine

All loans are issued based on the **Dynamic Discounted Present Value (dDPV)** of the collateral. This ensures that the protocol never over-extends credit against future unlocks.

`PV = Q * P * D`

- **Q**: Token quantity in escrow.
- **P**: Current market price (verified via decentralized oracles).
- **D**: Composite risk discount factor.

### Risk Discount Components
The discount factor `D` is a multi-variant function accounting for:
- **Time Decay**: Longer vesting horizons require deeper discounts to account for opportunity cost and long-term uncertainty.
- **Volatility Scaling**: Higher historical volatility triggers conservative LTV caps.
- **Liquidity Depth**: Market depth on major DEXs affects the liquidation buffer.
- **Protocol Health**: Global protocol parameters tuned by Vestra governance.

## 2. Methodology & Stress Testing

While the core logic is executed on-chain for transparency and speed, we perform rigorous off-chain simulations to ensure the protocol remains resilient in extreme market conditions ("Black Swan" events).

### Monte Carlo Calibration
We run thousands of simulated price paths per asset class to identify the **5th percentile outcome** over the vesting horizon. 
- **Low Volatility Assets**: Receive optimized LTV ratios and faster liquidity access.
- **High Volatility Assets**: Are subject to aggressive haircuts and time-sensitive borrowing limits.

### Safety Guardrails
1. **Deterministic Floor**: No loan is issued above the mathematically safe 5th percentile price outcome.
2. **Dynamic Decay**: Borrowing limits naturally contract as the unlock date nears, ensuring the final repayment remains over-collateralized.
3. **Governance Tuning**: Risk parameters are periodically updated by veVESTRA governors based on real-world performance data.

## 3. Implementation

Vestra maintains a "Risk-First" philosophy. By prioritizing the safety of the lending pool, we ensure that $VESTRA remains the most stable credit layer for illiquid assets in the ecosystem.
