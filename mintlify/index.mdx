# Vestra Protocol

Turning vested promises into present power. 

Vestra is a vesting-backed credit infrastructure designed for the next era of DeFi. We enable borrowers to unlock instant liquidity from vested tokens while providing lenders with secured access to vested asset yields and missed-sale upside.

- **[Whitepaper](/whitepaper)**: Technical deep-dive into protocol mechanics and deterministic underwriting.
- **[Risk Models](/risk-models)**: Detailed LTV simulations and collateral management logic.
- **[FAQ](/faq)**: Common questions about liquidity, security, and integration.

## Running locally (Mintlify)

1) Install the Mintlify CLI (one time): `npm i -g mint`  
2) From `/Vestra/mintlify`: `mint dev`  
3) Open http://localhost:3000 to preview.
