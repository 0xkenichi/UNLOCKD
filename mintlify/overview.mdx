# Protocol Overview

Vestra Protocol is a non-custodial credit infrastructure that enables borrowing against the deterministic value of vested and locked token claims. 

By collateralizing vesting schedules rather than circulating tokens, Vestra preserves long-term alignment between builders and ecosystems while providing immediate liquidity for founders, contributors, and DAOs.

## Market Segmentation

- **Borrowers**: Founders and contributors who need liquidity without selling their vested upside at a discount.
- **Lenders**: Institutional practitioners seeking secured, high-trust yield correlated to vesting schedules and missed-sale upside.
- **Ecoytsem**: DAOs and Treasuries that want to support talent without diluting their governance or market presence.

## Core Value Proposition

- **Vesting-Backed Credit**: No forced sales or price-based margin calls.
- **Deterministic Risk**: Underwriting is tied to time/unlock events rather than market volatility.
- **Non-Custodial Enforcement**: Escrow claim rights via on-chain smart contracts with settlement-at-unlock.
