# CRDT Tokenomics

This page summarizes the finalized Phase 1 token policy for CRDT. The canonical source remains `docs/TOKENOMICS_FINAL.md`.

## Token Specification
- Token name: CRDT
- Symbol: CRDT
- Standard: ERC-20 + ERC20Votes
- Decimals: 18
- Total supply: 1,000,000,000 CRDT (fixed cap)
- Inflation: none in Phase 1

## Allocation
- Treasury: 30%
- Team: 20%
- Protocol liquidity reserve: 20%
- Community sale: 15%
- Presale: 7%
- VC/investors: 5%
- Airdrop: 3%

## Vesting and Unlocks
- Team: 12-month cliff, then 36-month linear vesting
- VC/investors: 6-month cliff, then 24-month linear vesting
- Treasury: timelock-controlled, governance-approved emissions only
- Liquidity reserve: governance-approved pool requirements
- Presale: 10% at TGE, 90% linear over 12 months
- Community sale: 25% at TGE, 75% linear over 9 months
- Airdrop: staged claim windows with anti-sybil controls

## Treasury and Governance Controls
- Treasury mandate: audits, grants, growth, and risk buffer
- Spending path: on-chain governance proposal + timelock
- Quorum: 4% of circulating voting power
- Passing threshold: 55% yes votes
- Voting window: 5 days
- Timelock: 48 hours

## Guardrails
- No discretionary minting in Phase 1
- No off-path treasury transfers
- No unlock acceleration without supermajority governance
- Public unlock dashboard required before TGE

For full policy details and update cadence, refer to `docs/TOKENOMICS_FINAL.md`.
