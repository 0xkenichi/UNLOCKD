# UX: Identity & Governance Flow

Securing the protocol while empowering its users.

## Identity: The "Consented" Model
- **Privacy-Enhanced KYC**: For high-net-worth borrowers, identity is verified off-chain; the protocol only receives a ZK-proof of "Accredited Status."
- **Social Proof**: Integrating Web3 social scores to slightly boost `Omega` for known founders with a history of positive delivery.

## Governance: The Gauge Terminal
- **Visualizing Votes**: 3D heatmaps showing where protocol liquidity is currently directed.
- **Bribe Market Integration**: Native UI support for external projects to "bribe" `veCRDT` holders for liquidity allocation, mirroring the Curve/Convex model for institutional efficiency.
