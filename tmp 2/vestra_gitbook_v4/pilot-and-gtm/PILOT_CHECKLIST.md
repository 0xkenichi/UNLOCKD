# Pilot Checklist: Launch Gate

The formal checklist for onboarding the first institutional pilot partners.

## Legal & Compliance
- [ ] Partner LOI (Letter of Intent) signed.
- [ ] Regulatory "Pass-Through" research complete for partner's jurisdiction.
- [ ] Multi-sig whitelist updated for pilot-specific stablecoin pools.

## Technical Onboarding
- [ ] Custom `VestingAdapter` deployed for partner's native token.
- [ ] Omega Watcher "Seed Funding" analysis complete.
- [ ] Dedicated support channel established.

## Execution
- [ ] Pilot-specific `dDPV` parameters approved by Risk Committee.
- [ ] Initial liquidity ($1M+) seeded in partner vault.
