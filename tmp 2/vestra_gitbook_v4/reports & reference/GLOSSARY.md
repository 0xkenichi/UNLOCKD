# Glossary of Terms

- **dDPV**: Dynamic Discounted Present Value.
- **Omega (Ω)**: The predictive AI risk multiplier.
- **Inverted Curve**: A rate model where longer duration equals higher risk/rates.
- **Staged Auction**: A multi-phase liquidation occurring both pre- and post-unlock.
- **Stealth Address**: A non-linkable payout address derived via ERC-5564.
- **Strategic Recourse**: On-chain authority to seize native assets to cover deficits.
- **veCRDT**: Vote-Escrowed Resilience Token.
- **Vesting Adapter**: The logic layer connecting Vestra to external lockup contracts.
