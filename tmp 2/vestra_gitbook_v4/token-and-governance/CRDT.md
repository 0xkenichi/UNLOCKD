# $CRDT & veCRDT Tokenomics

The Vestra Protocol is powered by the **Protocol Resilience Token ($CRDT)**.

## Token Utility
- **veCRDT (Vote-Escrowed CRDT)**: Locks `$CRDT` for up to 4 years to receive governance power and revenue shares.
- **Revenue Capture**: 100% of the protocol's origination fees and interest spreads are used to market-buy `$CRDT` and distribute it to `veCRDT` holders.
- **Gauge Voting**: Governors vote on which collateral pools receive the most stablecoin liquidity.

## The Resilience Flywheel
1.  **Lending Volume Grows** -> Origination fees increase.
2.  **Revenue Market-Buys $CRDT** -> Upward pressure on token value.
3.  **veCRDT Yield Rises** -> Incentivizes more locking.
4.  **Protocol Liquidity Deepens** -> Better terms for borrowers.

## Governance Power
LTV caps and interest rate floors are ultimately proposed by the DAO. High-staked `veCRDT` holders are the primary guardians of the protocol's risk parameters.
