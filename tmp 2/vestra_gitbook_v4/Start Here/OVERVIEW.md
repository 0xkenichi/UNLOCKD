# Overview: The Sovereign Credit Primitive

Vestra Protocol is the first decentralized, non-custodial credit layer for **time-locked digital assets**. 

## The Mission
To restore economic velocity to $1.35T+ in "inert capital" (vesting tokens, locked claims, and pre-TGE allocations) while ensuring absolute safety for liquidity providers via mathematical rigor and predictive AI.

## Who We Serve
1.  **Founders & Core Contributors**: Unlock personal liquidity against long-term vesting schedules without selling tokens.
2.  **DAO Treasuries**: Hedge their own token's volatility or act as lenders to their own community, facilitating ethical buy-backs on defaults.
3.  **Institutional Lenders**: Access high-yield, fixed-income products secured by mathematically bounded risks and on-chain claims.
4.  **Strategic Liquidators**: Market makers and OTC desks looking for discounted entry into high-value token positions via pre-unlock auctions.

## Core Pillars
- **Non-Custodial Sovereignty**: Users retain ownership of their future; Vestra only holds the claim rights.
- **Recursive Risk Defense**: Continuous multivariate pricing (dDPV) ensures no loan is issued beyond its safety floor.
- **Privacy First**: Opt-in stealth address flow (ERC-5564) secures financial movements.
- **Zero-Deficit Guarantee**: Multi-layered liquidation buffers and staged auctions protect the protocol's solvency.
