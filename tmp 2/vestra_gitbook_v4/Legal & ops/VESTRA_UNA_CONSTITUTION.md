# Vestra UNA Constitution

**Vestra Unincorporated Nonprofit Association (UNA)**

## Article 1: Purpose
The Vestra UNA is established to govern, maintain, and expand the Vestra Protocol as a decentralized credit primitive for the benefit of the global Web3 community.

## Article 2: Non-Profit Status
The Association shall not operate for a profit. All protocol revenues must be used for the maintenance of the protocol, the $CRDT resilience flywheel, or the funding of ecosystem grants.

## Article 3: Membership
Membership is granted to any holder of `veCRDT`. Governance power is proportional to the amount and duration of the locked tokens.

## Article 4: The Risk Committee
A specialized body of members elected by the DAO to manage the protocol's safety parameters and emergency functions.
