# Legal Advisory Memo (Internal Only)

## Executive Summary
Vestra's core primitive (non-recourse lending against time-locked assets) occupies a novel regulatory space. This memo outlines the primary risks and mitigations.

## Key Risk Areas
1.  **Securities Law**: The $CRDT token utility is tied strictly to protocol revenue and governance. Counsel has advised on a "Resilience Token" framing to differentiate from speculative securities.
2.  **Lending Regulations**: By restricting the protocol to "Sophisticated Participants" and "Institutional Pilots" during the early phases, we minimize exposure to consumer protection laws.
3.  **Cross-Border Liability**: The UNA structure provides a layer of protection for the core contributors, establishing the protocol as a collective entity.

## Mitigation Strategy
- **Terms of Service**: Mandatory cryptographic signatures on all loan originations.
- **Strategic Recourse**: Framing asset seizure as a "Consented Liquidation Event" within a commercial contract.
