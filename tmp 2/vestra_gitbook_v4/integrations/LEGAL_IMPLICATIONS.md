# Legal Implications (Summary)

> [!NOTE]
> This information is internal research and does *not* constitute legal advice.

## Regulatory Status
Vestra Protocol operates as a decentralized primitive. 
- **Non-Custodial**: Users retain legal claim to their assets; the protocol only holds a cryptographic lien.
- **Lending Laws**: The protocol is designed to satisfy the "Non-Recourse Commercial Loan" framework in most common-law jurisdictions.

## Compliance Considerations
- **Stealth Addresses**: While providing user privacy, they do not circumvent AML/KYC requirements for institutional on-ramps. Vestra is a base-layer protocol; the burden of compliance lies with the individual user and their service providers.
- **Jurisdiction**: Mainnet deployment will be governed by the **Vestra Unincorporated Nonprofit Association (UNA)**.
