# Protocol Integrations

Building the credit layer for the wider Web3 stack.

## Use Case: DAO OTC Treasury Play
DAOs can act as Liquidity Providers for their own vested tokens.
- **Benefit**: If a contributor defaults, the DAO automatically "buys back" its own tokens at a math-based discount through the Staged Auction.
- **Outcome**: Non-custodial, non-inflationary treasury management.

## Ecosystem Partners (Targeted)
